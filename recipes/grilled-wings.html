Grilled Wings

Marinade overnight 1kg chicken mid-wings 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp tomato puree (double strength) 2 tbsp greek yoghurt 2 tsp minced garlic 1tsp red pepper flake 1 tsp cumin 1/2 tsp sumac 1/2 tsp urfa pepper flakes 2 tsp kosher salt Cook Cook on very high heat for a minimum of 8 minutes, turning once every minute. When possible make slits in the chicken. When cooking in oven, turn every 3 minutes on the highest heat for a total of 12 minutes.
