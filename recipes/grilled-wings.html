Grilled Wings

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken mid-wings
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree (double strength)
  • 2 tbsp greek yoghurt
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp red pepper flake
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp sumac
  • 1/2 tsp urfa pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp kosher salt

Cooking Instructions

  1. Marinade ingredients together. It can be left overnight but this doesn't make a difference
  2. Chicken should be at room temperature before cooking
  3. Cook on very high heat for a minimum of 8 minutes, turning once every minute. When possible make slits in the chicken.
  4. When cooking in oven, turn every 3 minutes on the highest heat for a total of 12 minutes.
  5. Halfway through cooking, make slits in the wings
