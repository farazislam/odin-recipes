Grilled Mackerel

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pat dry fillet with towel.
  2. Add grated garlic (one clove) and olive oil.
  3. Pre heat the cooking grate to prevent sticking
  4. Cook mackerel skin side down. Add salt and lemon juice to the flesh side. After 2 minutes, check if skin easily separates from grill (instead of sticking) and turn over if happy with the browning on the skin.
  5. Total cooking time should be about 4-6 minutes if it has good heat.
